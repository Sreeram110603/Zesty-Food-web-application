Our Menu

Classic Panipuri

Pani puri, A popular indian street food consisting of hollow,crispy puris filled with a mixture of flavored water,potatoes,onions and spices. It's typically served as a snack or appetizer and enjoyed for its burst of flavours and textures.

Cappucinno Delight

A Cappucinno is a type of coffee drink that originated in Italy.It's made with equal parts of espresso,steamed milk and milk foam. Cappucinno are often served in smaller cups and areknown for their rich flavour and frothy foam on the top.

Spicy French fries

French fries are popular side dish made from potatoes that are cut into thin strips and deep-fried until crispy. They are typically seasoned with salt and can vary in thickness and size.
